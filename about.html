Our Core Philosophy

Velocity over Volume

We don't "boil the ocean." We focus on selecting three high-impact projects that deliver measurable results within 90 days. We prove value early to secure the long-term mandate.

Economic Integrity

Every AI initiative must be tied to a specific P&L line item. If we can't show how a use case impacts revenue, margins, or working capital, it doesn't belong on the roadmap.

Structural Clarity

Building everything in-house is a mistake. Buying everything is a trap. We provide the structured criteria to help you navigate the Build vs. Buy vs. Partner landscape without expensive missteps.
